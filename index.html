Built for the work.

Three ideas, done carefully: fast logging, honest analysis, and consistency that survives real life.

Log

A set in seconds.

Weight, reps, RPE, rest — captured before the plates stop spinning. marble remembers your last set so repeating it is one tap, and a rest timer waits in the Dynamic Island.

  • Save + Next keeps you moving through a session.
  • Siri and the Action button log a set without opening the app.
  • Scan turns a handwritten page into logged sets, on-device.

Trends

Coached by your own numbers.

Every chart is computed from your logged sets, on your iPhone. No wearable scores, no black boxes — just an honest read on whether each lift is moving.

  • Estimated 1RM trends with a plain verdict: progressing, holding, or adapted.
  • A real PR feed — and a cue when a record is in reach today.
  • A monthly report with fair month-over-month comparisons, written privately on your phone.

Consistency

Streaks that forgive.

Set a weekly goal and build a streak that bends without breaking. Hit four weeks in a row and marble banks a flex week that absorbs a miss — comebacks are celebrated, not shamed.

  • Calendar shows every training day at a glance.
  • Weekly split maps your plan, Monday to Sunday.
  • One quiet reminder that cancels itself once you’ve trained.

Questions.

Is marble free?

Yes. Every feature is free — there’s nothing to unlock.

Does marble work offline?

Yes. Everything is stored on your iPhone. No account, no server, no sync to anyone else’s cloud.

Where does the coaching come from?

From your own logged sets. Estimated 1RM trends, PR detection, and the monthly report are all computed on your device.

Does marble work with Apple Watch or Garmin?

Yes — both arrive through Apple Health. marble imports their workouts, and can send your strength sessions back with an effort score so they count toward Apple’s Training Load.

Does marble show ads or sell my data?

No ads, no tracking, no selling. marble is local-only.
